Why am I interested in Cyberpunk Futurism?

I find it has many parallels to the way corporate structure and the first world is going now. Cyberpunk is a warning to what may happen if the government allows unchecked corporations to get out of hand. Unfortunately, this is evident during congress hearings on new technologies. In which they clearly lack the understanding of these new technologies. As well as the lack of any actual antitrust breakups in recent decades.

The pros of Cyberpunk.

Many advancements in recent human history have been mostly digital. Although, with the exponential advancement of Artificial Intelligence that will soon change. In that, the physical world will start to begin to change. New methods and procedures in material science are brought about because of the new age of accelerated tech. Redefining the capabilities of our societies.
